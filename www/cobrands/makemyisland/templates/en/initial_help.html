Using this app you can report problems such as:

  • dumping garbage on public areas (Male' Villimale' Hulhumale' and the 4 island stretch of Laamu Atolls).
  • Broken public street lights (Laamu Atoll only).
  • Erosion of the beaches (Laamu Atoll only).
  • Damage to public property (Laamu Atoll only).
  • Graffiti (for the 4 island stretch of Laamu atoll: Maandhoo, Kahdhoo, Gan and Fonadhoo).
  • Improper parking along the main road (Laamu Atoll only).

It works both online and offline.
